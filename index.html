ABOUT

Everything Must Go is a procedural cosmos consumed by a spinning black hole, reborn forever — stars, planets, galaxies, and a rare rogue merger, cycling from serene to carnage to true darkness before the next cosmos begins.

Both the visuals and the sound are procedurally generated: the score is fully synthesized WebAudio, no samples, driven live by the same cycle that drives the sim.
